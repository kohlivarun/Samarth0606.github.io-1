    Industrial Traning

    This ‘Placement guaranteed’ program (yes, that’s right, Placement guaranteed program) has been designed for those students who have their 8th semester earmarked for Industrial training. This program is not just for CS or IT students and even ECE, Mech, Automation, Electrical and Civil branch students can join the program. In this program, we will start as beginners, master Data structures and algorithms, practice questions and then learn and practice Full stack Web development. This program involves hands-on coding and working on live and capstone projects. After the program, students will be offered Placement drives of companies and they will be assisted to get placed in good IT firms. This will be a Classroom program conducted in both Pitampura and Noida centres and has limited seats.
